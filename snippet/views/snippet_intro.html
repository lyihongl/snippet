What is snippet?


Lets say you have worked some electrical engineering jobs, and some software jobs.

Thus you have a resume for eletrical engineering, and a software resume.

Now you are applying to a job that requires both electrical and software skills, and you need an updated resume.

Instead of digging through previous resumes and hacking them together,

you can upload each individual experience you've had to Snippets (tm)

and select the desired experiences to export onto a resume.
